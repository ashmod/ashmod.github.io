**Tech:** C++, CMake, Exiv2, TagLib, CLI

Privacy is paramount. Metasweep is a robust CLI tool that helps you:
- Inspect hidden metadata in various file formats.
- Strip metadata to protect your privacy.
- Batch process files efficiently.
- "Sweeping" your digital footprint clean.
